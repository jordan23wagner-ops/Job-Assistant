Welcome! Let's get you set up

Alicia uses a free key from Groq to power its AI. It takes about 2 minutes to get one — just follow these 3 steps.

1
Create a free Groq account Click the button below. Sign up with Google or email — it's free and no credit card is needed.
2
Create your key & copy it On the Groq page, click Create API Key, type any name (like "Alicia"), then click Copy. The key starts with gsk_
3
Paste your key below Paste it in the box and click Save. You only do this once.

Your key is saved only on this computer and is shared with no one except Groq.

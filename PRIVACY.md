# Privacy Policy — Alicia AI, Job Assistant

**Last updated: July 2026 (v1.7.0)**

## What stays on your device

Your saved resumes, application answers (including any EEO responses), learned
custom-question answers, site passwords, application tracker, and chat history are
stored locally in Chrome's extension storage on your device. They are never sold,
shared, or uploaded for their own sake.

## What is sent to our servers, and when

When you use an AI feature (chat, match scoring, resume tailoring, cover letters,
interview prep, custom-question answering), the text needed for that feature — your
message, the job posting's text, and/or relevant excerpts of your active resume — is
sent to our backend (chatwillow.com, hosted on Vercel) and forwarded to AI inference
providers solely to generate the response. We do not use this content for advertising
and we do not sell it. To enforce fair daily usage limits, the backend records an
estimate of processing time per account (or per hashed IP address for signed-out use).

## Accounts (optional)

Alicia works without an account. If you sign in for a higher daily allowance, we
collect your email address to send a one-time sign-in code (authentication is
provided by Supabase). Your session tokens are stored locally in extension storage,
and your subscription status (free or Pro) is stored in our database.

## Payments

Alicia Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Stripe on Stripe's hosted checkout
pages. The extension never sees or stores your card details.

## What we don't do

No ads, no analytics trackers, no selling or sharing of personal data, and the
extension never submits a job application on your behalf.

## Your control

Sign out at any time from Tools → Account. Uninstalling the extension removes all
locally stored data. To delete your account and associated server-side data, contact
us at the email below.
